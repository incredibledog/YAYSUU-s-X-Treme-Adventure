=============================================================================================================================
GETTING STARTED
YAYSUU's X-Treme Adventure runs on Windows 10/11.
If you're reading this, you have successfully extracted the game!
1. Launch the executable file named 'yaysuuxtreme.exe'
2. After a hopefully short while, the Incredible Dog Games logo will appear. You can press START to skip it if necessary!
3. When the YAYSUU's X-Treme Adventure logo appears, press START with your input method of choice and you are ready to play!
=============================================================================================================================
CONTROLS
You can rebind your keyboard controls in the OPTIONS menu under REBIND CONTROLS.
DEFAULT KEYBOARD CONTROLS
Arrow Keys - Up, Down, Left, Right
Z - Jump/Confirm
X - Dash/Back
C - Run/Delete
Enter - Start/Pause
GAMEPAD CONTROLS (Xbox layout)
Left Analog or D-Pad - Up, Down, Left, Right
A - Jump/Confirm
B - Dash/Back
X - Run/Delete
Start - Start/Pause
=============================================================================================================================
MAIN MENU
From here, you can access all that YAYSUU's X-Treme Adventure has to offer.
STORY - Play through the campaign of YAYSUU's X-Treme Adventure, and save the world! (Well, partially, in the demo.)
TRIAL - Replay past stages, and go for a high score or a record time!
OPTIONS - Tweak various options to your liking.
EXTRAS - Enter cheat codes, and check out extra content, like extra stages!
=============================================================================================================================
THE STORY SO FAR
In the small Easthill island, YAYSUU and Teddy live a quiet life in their house, with their beloved cat. But this doesn't sit well with one, very mean inventor... None other than their arch nemesis, FRINGLEBERT THEODORE!! One day, YAYSUU wakes up to discover his cat has been stolen! Venturing outside into the Chill Fields, he comes across a squad of Fringlebots claiming the fields as their own, slowly killing the wildlife and plants... Now, it's up to YOU to stop Fringlebert and save the world! ...Oh, and the cat, too, obviously.
=============================================================================================================================
CHARACTERS
YAYSUU - A fleet-footed, cheery stickling with attitude, YAYSUU's the star of the show, ready to thwart Fringlebert's plans! He can be rather loud at times, but he's a good person at his core.
TEDDY - A grumpy dude who has a more soft side when tending to his cat. He's more of a parkourist, with explosive power and a temper to match!
=============================================================================================================================
TIPS AND TRICKS
* It may seem tempting to hold RUN and dash through the stage, but remember to take it slow sometimes and focus! If you run without looking, you may get really hurt.
* In certain menus, you can press the RUN key to delete save data corresponding to that menu. Be careful when you do this, as this is irreversible!
* If you're unsure about a run, press START to pause the game and select RETRY from the pause menu. You'll restart right from the beginning of the stage!
* Check any suspicious spots in the stage for goodies and secrets. There's a good chance you'll find something worth your while.
* Complete the STORY MODE for an extra surprise!
